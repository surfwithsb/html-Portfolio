Shinjan's Top Picks for Best Movies of all Time

My Top 3 movies of All Time

Pather Panchali

A Satyajit Ray classic. This Bengali adapted screenplay based on the novel of Bangla literary icon Bibhutibhushan Bandopadhyay has stood the test of time. Set in rural Bengal, the film depicts the simple yet pain-stricken rural life in poverty. Depicts the loving bond between siblings and the art of finding joy even in abject poverty

Sonar Kella

This Satyajit Ray film is everything you can ask for if you are a fan of suspense, thrill and investigative plot.With a hint of humour through the iconic character of Jatayu and the heroism of Bengal's fav detective Feluda this is a film like no other.
